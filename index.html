⚠️ Mandatory Compliance

FBR Has Made Digital Invoicing Mandatory for All Businesses

July 1, 2025 marked a turning point in Pakistan's tax system. Under SRO 709(I)/2025 and SRO 69(I)/2025, all sales tax registered businesses must submit invoices electronically through FBR's IRIS system. Every sale must now generate a JSON invoice with a unique FBR number and QR code — automatically, in real-time.

Businesses that ignore this mandate face invoice invalidation (your buyers can't claim input tax), operational suspension, ATL blacklisting, and fines that escalate with each violation.

What is FBR IRIS integration and why do I need it? ▾
FBR IRIS (Integrated Revenue Information System) is Pakistan's centralized e-invoicing platform. Under Sales Tax Rule 150Q and SRO 709(I)/2025, all registered businesses must connect their invoicing system to IRIS. When you issue a sales tax invoice, it must be submitted to FBR in real-time, validated, and given a unique FBR Invoice Number — which must then appear on your customer's invoice. Without this, your invoices are legally invalid and your customers cannot claim input tax adjustments.
Why is PRAL integration better than other licensed integrators? ▾
PRAL (Pakistan Revenue Automation Limited) is FBR's official IT arm and provides integration services free of charge to all taxpayers. Other licensed integrators (there are about 30 approved ones) charge significant fees — sometimes PKR 1–5+ lakh — for the same IRIS connection. Our platform uses PRAL integration, passing those savings directly to you. You get the same FBR-compliant connectivity at a fraction of the cost.
What are the penalties for non-compliance? ▾
FBR has an escalating penalty structure: Rs. 500,000 for the first violation (not integrating), Rs. 1,000,000 for subsequent violations, and up to Rs. 3,000,000 for continued non-compliance. Additionally, Rs. 50,000 or 2% of tax (whichever is higher) per non-compliant invoice. Businesses can also be placed on the ATL blacklist — preventing access to government contracts, bank loans, and other benefits. As of November 2025, FBR had collected Rs. 2.3 billion in penalties.

What information must be included in an FBR digital invoice? ▾
FBR requires 12 mandatory master-level fields (Invoice Type, Sales Value, Total Retail Price, etc.) and 13 item-level fields per line item (including HS Code, Product Code, SRO Item Serial Number). The submitted invoice must return a unique 22-digit FBR Invoice Number, and the final customer invoice must include a QR code and this number. Our system auto-generates all required fields and handles the QR code — you just enter your invoice data.
